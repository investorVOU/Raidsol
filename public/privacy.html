PRIVACY_LOG

Data_Handling_Policy_v4.2  ·  Effective: 2025-01-01

_01

DATA_COLLECTION

The Solana Raid Protocol only sees your public wallet address and on-chain raiding history. We do not collect cookies, IP addresses, email addresses, or personal identity markers. You are an anonymous node. No account registration is required. No personal data is stored.

_02

BLOCKCHAIN_RECORDS

Every raid entry, attack, and extraction is recorded on the Solana blockchain. This data is permanent and public by nature — anyone can query it using a Solana explorer. The Solana Raid interface visualizes this public data. We do not control what is recorded on-chain.

_03

THIRD_PARTY_NODES

The app uses Supabase (supabase.com) for storing gameplay statistics such as raid scores and leaderboard rankings, linked only to your public wallet address. We also use Helius and Alchemy as Solana RPC providers; your wallet address may be included in RPC calls when fetching on-chain data. We do not share this data with advertisers or data brokers.

_04

TOKEN_DISTRIBUTIONS

This dApp distributes SOL and SPL tokens (including USDC and SKR) as in-game rewards directly to your connected Solana wallet. All distributions are on-chain and traceable. _05

CONTACT
